# The Great Hall

*Centuries of darkness have made this room their home. The banners rotted long ago. What remains are the bones of grandeur — and something that refuses to leave.*

---

## Description

The Great Hall stretches vast and cold beneath a ceiling lost in shadow. A shattered chandelier hangs by a single rusted chain, swaying without wind. The cracked stone floors are carpeted in ash and fragments of what was once finery.

At the far end, where a throne once stood, a figure drifts — barely visible against the dark. A knight in dissolving armor, bound by chains of pale light that pulse in rhythm with something deep beneath the castle.

His hollow lantern-eyes find you immediately.
