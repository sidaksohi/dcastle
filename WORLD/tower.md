# The Watchtower

*High above the curse, the tower breathes differently. Something was placed here deliberately — hidden, but not lost.*

---

## Description

A circular chamber at the top of a long winding stair. Arrow slits look out over the dead landscape surrounding the castle — grey hills, leafless trees, a sky the color of old pewter.

In the center of the room, a stone pedestal holds a single object. The rest of the tower is stripped bare — no furniture, no remnants, nothing except dust and the object on the pedestal. As if whoever placed it here removed every distraction to make its importance clear.

The air up here is different. Thinner. Older. As if time forgot to rot this room.
