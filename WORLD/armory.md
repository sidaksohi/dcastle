# The Armory

*Once the pride of the castle garrison. Now a mausoleum of broken weapons and shattered purpose.*

---

## Description

Weapon racks line the walls, most holding only splinters or fingers of rust where blades once lived. The floor is deep in broken links of chainmail, the remnants of armor no one will ever wear again. Everything here smells of iron oxide and damp rot.

One weapon survives intact — barely. A sword propped against the far wall, its blade pitted and brown but its edge still dangerous in the way that only neglected things can be.
