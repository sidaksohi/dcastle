# Dungeon Cell

*Whoever was kept here was meant to be forgotten.*

---

## Description

A low, damp cell carved from the living rock beneath the castle. Iron rings are bolted to the walls at wrist and ankle height. The floor is black with old moisture that never fully dries. A single barred window near the ceiling shows nothing but more stone and deeper dark.

The silence here has weight.

On the floor, half-buried in grime and centuries of sediment, something metal catches a sliver of light that has no business reaching this far down.
