# Castle Entrance

*The great iron gate stands behind you, fused shut by decades of rust and malice. Before you, the castle yawns open — cold, dark, and patient.*

---

## Description

You stand in the entrance hall of a vast and decaying castle. Stone columns flank a path northward into shadow. To your east, a narrow corridor slopes downward toward some lower passage. The flagstones beneath your feet are worn smooth by centuries of footsteps — few of which ever returned this way.

Above the sealed gate, carved into the lintel in a script that predates the castle's name: **"What enters does not leave unchanged."**

The air carries the smell of wet stone and old fear.

---

## Items Here

- **Torch** — A guttering torch hangs in a rusted sconce near the gate. Its flame is weak but persistent.

---
